Wastewater Management

Wastewater is water whose physical, chemical or biological properties have been changed as a result of the introduction of certain substances which render it unsafe for some purposes such as drinking. The day to day activities of man is mainly water dependent and therefore discharge ‘waste’ into water. Some of the substances include body wastes (faeces and urine), hair shampoo, hair, food scraps, fat, laundry powder, fabric conditioners, which can make people ill and damage the environment. Low-flow faucets

Low-flow faucets are fixtures designed to reduce water consumption by limiting the flow rate of water. They are an essential component of water conservation efforts in homes, businesses, and public facilities.
